* two

*     three
.
<ul>
<li>one</li>
<li>two</li>
<li>three</li>
</ul>
.

dashes work
.
- one
- two
- three
.
<ul>
<li>one</li>
<li>two</li>
<li>three</li>
</ul>

</ol>
